About Lambton College

Lambton College has been operating a public private partnership with Queen's College since 2017 and has successfully graduated over 4,000 international students. Currently, classes are composed of only international students. The Queen's College campus is located at 121 Brunel Road and includes 33,000 square feet of classrooms, labs, student areas and administration offices in two buildings. If you want to know more about the college visit college’s website.
