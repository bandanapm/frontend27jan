The Steps of Preparing Samay Baji

Samay baji (Nepal Bhasa: समय् बजि) is an authentic traditional dish of Newar community in Nepal. Here are the steps that are involved in preparing all the food items included in Samay Baji.

  1. Soak beaten rice (Chyura) in water for a few minutes to soften it.
  2. Marinate the buffalo meat (Chhwela) with spices and grill it until cooked.
  3. Prepare the wa: by making a batter of black gram paste or mung beans paste, and cook small pancakes on a griddle.
  4. Boil the eggs and fry them until they turn golden brown.
  5. Cook the black soybeans (Bhatamaas) until tender.
  6. Mix boiled beans with spices (Bodi ko Achar) and season it with flavors like mustard oil, fenugreek seeds, and turmeric powder.
  7. Make a spicy potato salad (Aalu-Wala) by mixing boiled potatoes with spices and herbs.
  8. Garnish the Samay Baji plate with finely cut ginger (Palu) for added flavor.
  9. Serve the Samay Baji with green leaves (Saag) as a side dish.
  10. Pair the dish with Ayla, an alcohol specific to the Newar Community, for a complete traditional experience.
